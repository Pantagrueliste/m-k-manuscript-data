
For ridding bone or horn of fattiness

One needs to make them boil in quicklime, and it must always
have water, and take a very clean pan. And when they are
washed, make them boil in the ashes of vine shoots, and
put them inside the hottest pot you can, and put
verdigris into the goldsmith's water
pot, and leave for the space of some time, and you will see a very
beautiful green color.
