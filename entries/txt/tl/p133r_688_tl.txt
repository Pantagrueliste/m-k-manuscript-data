
Feet of smalls lizards for
gold and silver

Because the feet of these, which are very small, are very delicate,
as you molded the top and d uncovered the belly
& the bottom of the feet, cover them lightly with wax &
then cast the second mold. And the wax being removed, it
leaves a g little gate for the aforesaid feet. And
should the bottom of these come of in one piece, you can repair it. And
the scales from the top of the toes will come out well.
